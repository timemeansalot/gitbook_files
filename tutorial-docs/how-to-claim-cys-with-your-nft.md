# How to claim CYS with your NFT

This guide walks you through the entire process—from connecting your wallet, checking your eligibility, confirming your shares, to claiming your CYS rewards.

## Connecting Your Wallet

To get started, visit [https://cysicfoundation.org/userPortal](https://cysicfoundation.org/userPortal) and click the **"Sign In"** button to connect your wallet. A prompt will appear asking you to approve the connection request. Once approved, your wallet address will be displayed on the interface, confirming a successful connection.

## Checking Your NFT & Shares

After connecting your wallet, you can:

* View your **NFT(s)**
* Check **eligibility**
* See the **total amount of CYS tokens** available for you to claim

You can also find the complete claim **rules** at the top of the page.

## Understanding the Rules

Once your shares are confirmed:

* **50%** of your CYS reward will be distributed **immediately**
* The remaining **50%** will unlock **linearly over 6 months**

## Confirm Your Shares

If your are eligible, you’ll see an active **“Unlock”** button. Click it to send a transaction confirming your shares.

> **Note:** You need to confirm your shares **both on BNB Chain and Base Chain**.

Upon confirmation, you’ll instantly receive **half** of your total rewards.

## Claim Your Rewards

After unlocking your shares, you can claim your remaining CYS tokens **anytime** as they continue to **unlock linearly** over the 6-month period.
